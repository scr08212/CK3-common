Domiciles are available to characters whose government type goes not require them to own landed titles. The appearance of buildings in a domicile depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the domicile is located. There are five types of domiciles:
* {{icon|governor efficiency}} Administrative governments have an [[Administrative estate]]
* [[File:Adventurer.png|24px]] Adventurer governments have a [[Camp]]
* {{iconify|Nomadic}} governments have a [[Nomadic capital]]
* {{iconify|Ritsuryō}} and {{iconify|Sōryō}} governments have a [[Manor]]
* {{iconify|Celestial}}, {{iconify|Meritocratic}} and {{iconify|Meritocratic Khanate}} governments have a [[Celestial estate]]
